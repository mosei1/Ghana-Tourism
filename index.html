Welcome to Ghana

Ghana is a country located in the West of Africa. The land was inhabited since 4000 B.C. It was known as the Gold Coast before it gained independence in 1957. It is the first country in Africa to gain independence in 1957. Ghana has a population of 29.4 million people. There are 10 regions in Ghana. There are 250 languages are spoken in Ghana. It is one of the most stable economies in West Africa and it is politically stable. One of the revenue generators in Africa and most specifically Ghana is tourism. Africa is a rich continent that has many natural vegetative covers as well as a very diverse wildlife. This website will highlight just a few of the beautiful tourist attractions in Ghana.
