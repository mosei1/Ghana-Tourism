Do you know Elmina Castle?

Elmina Castle is a whitewashed castle on the coast of the Central region of Ghana. It was once the largest European building constructed in the tropical part of Africa. It has a lot of history. Even though the location, beauty and the grandeur of the castle is something to marvel at, it has a very dark history that holds a somewhat dark cloud over the place. Elmina Castle was the last place that thousands of slaves were held before being taken across the ocean. Many horrific events happened in that castle.
