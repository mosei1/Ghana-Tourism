Do you know Mole National Park?

Mole National Park is a national park located in Ghana. It is the largest most developed protected area in the whole of Ghana. It is located in the West Gonja District and located mainly in the Northern region of Ghana. There are over 335 identified bird species. The mammals include some 500 elephants, 2000 antelopes and also many others.
