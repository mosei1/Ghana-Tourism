Do you know Boti Waterfalls?

Boti Falls is located just 17km North-east of Koforidua, the eastern regional capital. It is just over 30 minutes drive from Koforidua and over 90 minutes from Accra depending on your means of transportation. River Pawnpawn which forms the falls takes it source from Ahenkwa-Amalakpo before falling over an igneous rock outcrop at the Boti Langmase that is how the falls get its name. The fall was hidden in the forest until it was discovered by a white Catholic priest. It is recounted by locals that the priest used the base of the falls as an entertainment ground for his friends and himself.
